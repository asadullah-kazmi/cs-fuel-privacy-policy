CS Fuel Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CS Fuel handles information when you use the mobile app. CS Fuel is designed to help you manage vehicle fuel fill-ups, monitor consumption trends, and review your fuel history with privacy-conscious defaults.

Company CORDEROSERVICES S.A.S.
Android Package com.corderoservices.csfuel
Policy Version 1.0
Last Updated 03/21/2026

1. Overview

CS Fuel is a mobile application that helps users track fuel fill-ups for one or more vehicles. This Privacy Policy describes what information is handled by the app, why it is used, and what choices you have.

Our current product design stores core fuel and vehicle information locally on your device. We do not state or support selling your personal data.

2. Who We Are

The CS Fuel app is provided by CORDEROSERVICES S.A.S. ("we", "us", or "our").

Company details:

  • Address: Schaumburg, ILLINOIS, US
  • Support Email: contact@corderoservices.com

3. Scope

This policy applies to:

  • The CS Fuel Android app (package ID: com.corderoservices.csfuel).
  • This public privacy policy website used for transparency and compliance.

This policy does not automatically apply to third-party websites or services linked from our app or support channels. Those services have their own privacy terms.

4. Information We Collect

We collect and process information based on what you enter and how the app is configured.

CS Fuel does not currently require account registration for core usage, and core records are designed to remain local to your device unless you explicitly share data (for example, through support contact or future export features).

4.1 Information You Enter in the App

  • Vehicle data (for example: name, model, plate/identifier, fuel type).
  • Fuel log data (for example: fill-up date, volume, price, odometer, notes).
  • Dashboard-related records generated from your entries (aggregated statistics).

4.2 App Configuration Data

  • Preferences and settings stored locally on your device.
  • UI and usage preferences required for app behavior.

4.3 Support Communications

If you contact us, we may receive the details you provide in your message (such as email address, issue description, and attachments).

5. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide core app functionality for fuel tracking and vehicle management.
  • Generate dashboard insights and fuel statistics inside the app.
  • Store and retrieve your fuel history and settings locally on your device.
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot reported issues.
  • Improve performance and reliability if optional diagnostics are added later.

6. Legal Basis

Depending on where you live, our legal basis for handling information may include:

  • Your consent (for optional features where consent is required).
  • Performance of a contract (providing requested app functionality).
  • Legitimate interests (maintaining app security and support operations).
  • Compliance with legal obligations, when applicable.

Applicable privacy rights and requirements can vary by jurisdiction. This policy is intended to be interpreted in line with the laws of JURISDICTION and other laws that may apply to users in their location.

7. Storage and Security

CS Fuel currently stores core app data locally on your device through local storage or a local database. App settings and preferences are also stored locally.

By default, we do not claim continuous remote synchronization of your local fuel and vehicle records to company-controlled cloud servers.

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to reduce risk, including secure development practices and controlled access to support channels. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device (for example, passcode, biometric lock, and OS updates).

8. Retention

Because core app records are stored locally, retention is largely controlled by you. Your data generally remains on your device until you edit, delete, reset, or uninstall the app.

If support communications are received, we may keep those records for as long as needed to resolve requests, comply with legal obligations, or defend legal claims.

9. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share your app-entered fuel and vehicle records for advertising sale purposes.

Limited sharing may occur only when necessary, such as:

  • Service providers involved in support or infrastructure, if adopted in the future.
  • Legal requests, court orders, or compliance with applicable law.
  • Protection of rights, safety, and security of users, our company, or others.
  • Business transfer scenarios (for example, merger or acquisition), as allowed by law.

If optional analytics or crash reporting tools are enabled in the future, this section will be updated with provider names, data categories, and user choices.

10. International Transfers

The app's core records are stored locally on your device. If we later use external providers (such as support, analytics, or crash tools), information may be processed in countries outside your own.

In those cases, we will take reasonable steps to apply appropriate safeguards required by law in JURISDICTION and any applicable cross-border transfer rules.

11. Children's Privacy

CS Fuel is not directed to children under the age threshold defined by applicable law in the user's jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in ways that require parental authorization under applicable law.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through support channels, contact us at contact@corderoservices.com so we can review and take appropriate action.

12. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold through support channels.
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Request deletion, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing in specific circumstances.

Because core CS Fuel records are locally stored, you can directly manage much of your information inside the app by editing records, deleting entries, or uninstalling the app.

To submit a privacy request, contact contact@corderoservices.com and include sufficient details for us to respond.
